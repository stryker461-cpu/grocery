Snan Grocery is a small shop next to the Wasit Wetland Centre in Muwafja — nothing fancy, just fresh produce, everyday essentials, and someone behind the counter who's usually there before you are.

Most people in the area use it the same way: a quick run for what's missing from the fridge, or a full weekly order sent over on WhatsApp when they'd rather not leave the house.
